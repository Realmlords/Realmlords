Dominion and Scales

    Dominion

    Dominion is the strength of your pretender’s influence on a given region. This spreads as their influence spreads, temples constructed, deeds extolled, and Thrones captured. While within your own pretenders dominion

    • all troops gain +1 Morale
    • gain the benefits of positive Scales
    • disciples are automatically blessed and empowered

    In unfriendly dominion

    • all troops suffer -1 Morale
    • the effects of positive Scales do not aid your efforts
    • disciples loose a portion of their power

    Scales

    The Scales of the universe tip, seeking new equilibrium. While various regions, cultures, and situations are varied across the realm the influence of your pretender defines a new standard of normalcy. Each region has a set of Scales that affect all within it. As your dominion spreads the Scales of regions it encompasses will come to more closely mirror that of your pretender. Keep in mind also that the positive effects of Scales usually only apply to those who are aligned with the Dominion in the region.

    For example a given region might be Heat 1, Misfortune 1, Chaos 2. A warm and lawless land, yet as your dominion spreads these will shift over time.

    Heat vs Cold

    The natural opposition between Summer and Winter. These Scales affect the overall climate of a region. Their primary effect is to increase fatigue of those unused to fighting in such an environment.

    Heat 3

    • Fire damage increased by 3

    Fortune vs Misfortune

    What whims do the fates have in this new balance? Towards weal? Or woe?

    Luck 3

    • Random encounters are rolled twice, choosing either option
    • 2/day may re-roll any roll and keep the better result
    • Common occurrence of useful serendipity

    Luck 2

    • Random encounters are rolled twice, choosing either option
    • 1/day may re-roll any roll and keep the better results
    • Occasional beneficial events seem to occur

    Luck 1

    • 1/day may re-roll any roll and keep the better results

    Misfortune 1

    • Rolls of natural 1 result in critical fumbles

    Misfortune 2

    • Random encounters are rolled twice, tending to favor the worse outcome
    • Rolls of natural 1 result in critical fumbles

    Misfortune 3

    • Random encounters are rolled twice, tending to favor the worse outcome
    • Rolls of natural 1 result in critical fumbles
    • Circumstances consistently contrive to your undoing
