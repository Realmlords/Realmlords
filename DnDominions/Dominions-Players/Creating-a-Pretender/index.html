Creating a Pretender

    The pantokrator is dead and the world stands on the precipice. Will it fall into decay and ruin or will another god rise to power and found a new pantheon? You follow one such pretender god, a divine being of great potency and wisdom who seeks to claim the Thrones of Ascension and become the new Omnipotens.

    As a disciple you have a sliver of divine power, having received grand visions during the Age of Calamity which resulted in the pantokrator’s death. In this new age you will need to spread the word of your pretender.

    With your group decide

    • the name of your pretender god and their titles
    • one or more forms your god takes
    • allocate 4 points of scales
    • allocate 6 points of magic
    • spend 3 bonus points on either scales or domains
    • create their Blessing

    Scales

    Scales are opposing forces of the universe which your pretender seeks to rebalance in their image. Wherever your pretender spreads their dominion the laws of the universe shift towards their true nature. See Dominion and Scales for their exact effects.

    Scales come in positive and negative sides, allocate between -3 to +3 to each scale, for a total of 4 points. The exception to this is Heat vs Cold, both of which cost points to increase.

    • Heat vs Cold - the turning of the seasons as summer opposes winter
    • Fortune vs Misfortune - how the Fates pluck the strings of mortals
    • Growth vs Death - vibrancy and life opposed by decay
    • Order vs Chaos - the oldest battle between Law and Chaos
    • Industry vs Sloth - innovation and drive against a stagnating cosmos

    For example a pretender might have Cold (2), Misfortune (-1), Order (2), and Growth (1) for a total of 4 points spent.

    Magic

    Pretenders have secret knowledge concerning the operation of the universe, yet this knowledge is incomplete prior to their ascension. Allocate 6 points between the Paths of Magic which determine the domains your pretender presides over. This determines special magic their priests have access to, grants you points for blessings, and makes accessing rituals of that domain easier for mages in their service.

    • A score of 2 or higher unlocks those paths during character creation for your disciple
    • 3 or higher allows a blessing to include powers from that path
    • 4 or higher gives priests special effects when Smiting or Banishing Undead

    The paths are

    • Fire
    • Air
    • Water
    • Earth
    • Astral
    • Death
    • Nature
    • Blood

    Bonus Points

    Add 3 additional points, either as greater Scales or more Paths.

    Blessings

    Each pretender has a unique blessing they grant to those that attain favor. As a disciple you are automatically blessed when in your pretenders Dominion. Otherwise priests are able to grant these blessings temporarily to those deemed worthy (Sacred units only).

    Each point in a Path of Magic your pretender has gives you a corresponding point to spend on blessings, with a minimum score of 3 to have any blessings of that path. So a pretender with Fire 4, Nature 3, Astral 2 would pick four points of fire blessings and three points of nature blessings. Some blessings require points from multiple paths, as noted in their description.
