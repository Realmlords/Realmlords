# Fighter
May either use regular fighter or the Robby houserules (https://docs.google.com/document/d/1TLPth8PTEjsvtdJ-PDPn_DknSi8fcFYBRETH0cNxfJY/edit) fighter.

# Paladin
Smite Evil instead operates on any followers of another pretender, and all outsiders and undead. Ineffective against any unaligned individuals.

No longer gain access to Vancian spells (but may take archetypes which trade out this feature). 

Loose channel energy, instead gain access to tier 1 [[DnDominions/Dominions Players/Divine Power|Divine Magic]] at 3rd level. Tier 2 at 9th level.

# Ranger
Must take an archetype which trades out spells, having no access to Vancian spellcasting. 

At 4th level rangers gain access to Nature 1 if their pretender has at least one point in this path. At 8th level this increases to Nature 2.
