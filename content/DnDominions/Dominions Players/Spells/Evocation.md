> [!spell] Hand of Flame
> **School:** Evocation 1
> **Path:** Fire 1
> **Rarity:** Common
> **Casting Time:** 1 round
> **Cost:** 5
> **Range:** self
> With the flick of an upturned palm the caster conjures a flame in their hand. This shines as bright as a torch and continues to burn so long as the caster pays the cost of the spell each minute to feed the fire. The flame can also be hurled as a ranged attack, the same distance as a stone, which deals 1d6 + 2 x Fire rating damage. 
> - Fire 2: Relights after being thrown
