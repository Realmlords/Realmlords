# Magic Items
Most characters can use one set of boots, armor, headpiece, and up to two other magic items.

Forging a magic item requires
- 1 campaign turn
- knowledge of how to craft the item (typically the appropriate level in Construction school)
- minimum Path ratings
- expending gems

Unless otherwise noted magic items
- resize to fit their wielder
- are obviously supernatural
- bear the sigil of their maker

## Construction 0 (Trinkets)
Trinkets are magic items which any wizard can craft, forming the 0th level of the Construction school. Like other magic items they require the same amount of time to forge as a ritual spell.
