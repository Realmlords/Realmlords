## Thaumaturgy
> [!spell] Channeling
> **School:** Thaumaturgy
> **Source:** (Varies) 1
> **Occlusion:** Common
> **Casting Time:** Special
> **Cost:** 20
> **Range:** self
> There exists a version of this spell for each source of power. By casting this spell just before working a different magic the caster temporarily increases their potency by 1, up to a maximum of 3. In order to benefit from this the casting time of this spell must be made to match that of the spell to benefit from this bonus.
