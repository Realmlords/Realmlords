Those with *truespeech* are able to communicate with nearly anyone else they come across who use spoken language. Those hearing them understand their words as being from their own speech.
- cannot tell any direct untruths, and most attempts at subterfuge sound somewhat false
- unable to communicate with creatures of Chaos
- does not grant any particular understanding of written language, or nonverbal modes of communication
- must intentionally be attempting to speak a specific language, meaning that someone with *truespeech* would not be able to address everyone in a bilingual crowd at the same time
- no special understanding of idiom, culture, context, or history - which may make some communication less effective

> [!info] Truesign? 
> Some speculate that *truespeech* is but one aspect of a greater unity of the cosmos that would allow communication with any that the user comes across, including those who speak with signing or other forms of language. Additionally it may even allow the understanding and creation of written works, although others feel that this would be impossible due to fundamental limits of the nature of Law itself.
